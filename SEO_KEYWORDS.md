## SEO Copy Rules

- Write for homeowners first, Google second.
- Use city names naturally.
- Avoid keyword stuffing.
- Put the main keyword in the H1, title tag, meta description, intro, and one or two headings.
- Add FAQs to support long-tail search.
- Add internal links from blog posts to service pages.
- Add project proof and reviews to service pages.
- Mention nearby service areas naturally.
